How to Reach BioNEST @ UoH
  1. By Air: Take a Flight to "Shamshabad Airport" (Airport Code HYD) in Hyderabad from anywhere in the world, and take an Airport Taxi (Meru, Sky, Ola, Uber, or Private) and inform driver "University of Hyderabad after Gachibowli Stadium". Use Outer Ring Road from the Airport, and take exit to "Gachibowli" on the Nehru ORR. After toll, take a left turn at the Gachibowli Flyover, drive ~3KM. After passing IIIT and Gachibowli Stadium on the left, you will then reach within 1-2km, you will reach the main gate of University of Hyderabad. After obtaining relevant permissions, you reach School of Life Sciences by driving through the campus (South Campus). BioNEST is located on the 3rd floor of School of Life Sciences.
